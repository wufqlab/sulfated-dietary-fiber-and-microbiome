This is the script for transcriptomics and microbiome analysis (ex vivo and in vitro). 

fig 4: Transcriptomics for cells treated with kanamycin and/or fucoidan (volcano plots, cluster, pca, Interaction analysis);
fig 5: Fucoidan protects human fecal communities from antibiotics ex vivo (16s amplicon sequencing data analysis);
fig 6: Fucoidan facilitated the recovery of gut microbes in mice after antibiotic treatment (analysis of the longitudinal mice fecal microbiome dynamics prior, during, and after kanamycin treatment).

Details and results can be found from the paper: 
Wu, F., Yu, X.A., Angeles-Albores, D. et al. Sulfated dietary fiber protects gut microbiota from antibiotics. Microbiome 13, 183 (2025). https://doi.org/10.1186/s40168-025-02176-w
